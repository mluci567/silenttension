silent tension welcomes a key figure in the techno history books, detroit-born, berlin based alan oldham (dj t-1000). considered to be “detroit techno’s renaissance man”, oldham is an electronic musician, label owner, remixer, graphic illustrator and veteran international dj. with a “no-nonsense brand of bangin’, funky-ass techno”, this is not a show to miss. with local support from some of the cities finest techno selectors, ty bonlando and shannon hart warming up the the room.
